About Me

Hi, I'm Praveen Manimaran. I recently graduated with a Master’s degree in Data Science from UC San Diego, after completing my undergraduate studies in Statistics and Data Science at UC Santa Barbara. My journey into data began with a love for statistics, which gradually evolved into a deep passion for machine learning, artificial intelligence, and solving real-world problems through data-driven insights.

I'm currently a Data Science Intern at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, where I develop scalable analytics workflows, build ML pipelines for anomaly detection, and support experimental data analysis for high-impact scientific projects. This hands-on experience has strengthened my skills in MLOps, data architecture, and model deployment—giving me a front-row seat to the real-world applications of AI and big data.

Feel free to explore my work, and if you’d like to connect or collaborate, don’t hesitate to reach out. Let’s build something impactful together!
